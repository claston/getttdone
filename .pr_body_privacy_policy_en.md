## Summary

This PR adds a dedicated Privacy Policy page for the public website and links it from the landing footer.

- Added `frontend/politica-de-privacidade.html` with LGPD-oriented privacy sections in Portuguese.
- Added page-specific assets: `frontend/privacy-policy.css` and `frontend/privacy-policy.js`.
- Added a footer link in `frontend/index.html` to the new page.
- Added footer link styling in `frontend/ofx-landing.css` for visual consistency.

## Why

Users need a clear, accessible Privacy Policy page describing data handling, retention, security measures, and privacy contact channel.
